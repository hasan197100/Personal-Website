About Me

I am an Assistant Professor (Tenure Track) in the Mechanical Engineering and Advanced Manufacturing Program at Navajo Technical University. My research focuses on manufacturing product design using assembly design theories such as mereotopology and spatiotemporal behavior of mechanical products. I have a Ph.D. in Industrial and Systems Engineering from Wayne State University, and my work has been published in numerous prestigious journals and conferences.
